Overview

Through its unique technology understanding supported with IT, automation and analytics competences, AFRY is able to help clients significantly boost business performance and make autonomous operations reality

Bringing competences together is vital

  • Technical process understanding is the key for anybody wanting to carry out performance services in the digital format
  • Most companies lack the technical process understanding and therefore struggle in developing data analytics or advanced control concepts for industrial processes
  • Success requires bringing together experts in all five fields: this can provide very unique value
  • Managing inter-disciplinary teams is not easy, but we have already delivered success with this setup in a few projects
Our scope

Data analytics

  • Describes all imaginable ways to utilise data and analytics to create understanding of process performance against its achievable analytical optimum

IT-OT technology/architecture

  • IT-OT integration, technology and architecture are an enabler for digital process optimisation: stands for a platform that has an access to all data, makes it possible to create advanced algorithms (incl. AI), and enables process control through Industrial Edge

Management systems

  • The desired impact on performance is achieved through a formal management process, with clearly defined KPIs and responsibilities, reporting, meetings and action planning
  • ISO 50001

Advanced Process controls

  • Advanced controls that automate human decision making across all levels on process control hierarchy
